The college is affiliated to Choudhary Ranjit Singh University, Jind and recognized by NCTE. S.L.D. Girls College of Education constantly strives for quality education through highly qualified and experienced and devoted faculty, including visiting professors, experts lecturers and highly equipped library with an immense variety of books, journals and periodicals. The impeccably teacher training module has lead to grooming of professional personnels who are devoting savvy services at institute of education at primary and secondary levels. It definitely shall not be exaggeration to aver that SLD shall be a comprehensive institute of a variety of courses in the years to come.
SLD has been envisioned to introduce and initiate future teachers through the process of transformation into evolved beings who can lead and guide the children of tomorrow towards the knowledge century in a true sense. With holistic training components, value education and skill-orientation as the mainstay of the teacher education, SLD has a vision of empowering the nation and the world with teachers who can take the onus of shaping the next generation of students with a sense of responsibility for the nation and an orientation for internationalism.
Teaching at SLD emphasizes grooming students for surviving in this globally competitive world and become world class teachers. To achieve the goal of providing quality education, various innovative practices are used as transactional strategies. Students are provided with real life experiences. Special emphasis is laid on school experience program. Under the supervision of expert faculty, our students are provided with rich school experience where they can polish their teaching competencies. The institute has taken up the process of modernization through the infusion of ICT and promotion of research in the field of education.
