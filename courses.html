Registration Procedure
The students seeking admission at SLD have to fill-up the proscribed forms (given in the prospectus accompained by the required testimonials. The applicants under management quota will selected on the basis of academic achievements, merits and an interview conducted by college board. The applicants under the normal category will be admitted on the basis of merit list provided by Maharishi Dayanand University, Rohtak after short -listing through their entrance examination. The reservation norms shall be followed in accordance to the policy of state government.
 
Fee Structure
The fee will be charged as per the rules of State Govt./M.D. University, Rohtak; in the form Challan (Triplicate Challan)/Demand Draft in the favour of S.L.D. Girls college of Education, Palwal payable at Bank of Baroda, Palwal.
 
Identity Card
Each student will get an Identity-Card from the college which he/she is supposed to keep all the time while he/she is inside the campus. The students found without identity-Card shall be heavy panalized.
 
Character Certificate
Character Certificate shall be issued to the candidate only after completion of 6 months of the programme she is enrolled in. The certificate shall be issued once during the tenure of the course.
